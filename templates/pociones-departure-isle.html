All Wizard Alchemy Potions (Departure Isle)

First, you must go to the potion-making area to be able to make each of the potions you need. Keep in mind that the potion you want is not guaranteed due to the creation percentages.

Green circles appear indicating a higher percentage of potion crafting; these help us obtain the most difficult potions and have a lower percentage.

To use these green areas you need the cauldron that the traveler sells you at a camp you find during your adventure.

Ice Spike Potion

Ice Spike Potion

 Effects:

Shoots ice spikes at the target, dealing damage to opponents hit.

description:

Just like it's been taken straight out of the fridge, a summer essential.

Is Ice Spike Potion Good to Use?

It's a potion that's very good for the initial phase of the game; it's balanced in cooldown, since it charges quickly and has sufficient damage.

Wind Blade Potion

Wind Blade Potion

 Effects:

Shoots wind blades at the target, dealing damage to opponents hit.

description:

Very bitter, do not try lightly.

Is Wind Blade Potion Good to Use?

It's a very good potion for the start of an adventure; it has the shortest cooldown but lower damage than other potions, but the more you use it, the more damage you'll surpass the other potions.

Earth Shield Potion

Earth Shield Potion

 Effects:

Summons a wall of dirt that can block enemy attacks.

description:

Rumored to have health benefits, recommended to carry some.

Is Earth Shield Potion Good to Use?

The Earth Shield Potion is a good potion to have as a starter. The spells are a decent upgrade compared to the Common potions, and it is a good spell to use until you get a higher rarity wand that provides more damage and stat increase.
